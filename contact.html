Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment by calling us at +1 (234) 567-8900, emailing us at appointments@delasalasdental.com, or by creating an account on our website.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring your ID, insurance information (if applicable), and any previous dental records. Also, arrive 15 minutes early to complete paperwork.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major dental insurance plans. Please contact us to verify your specific insurance coverage before your appointment.

What are your emergency procedures?

For dental emergencies during business hours, call us immediately. For after-hours emergencies, call our emergency hotline at +1 (234) 567-8902.
